Ash Wanders

First the journey, then the connection.

My photography journey began in 2010 when I bought my first DSLR in Tokyo and commited to make the most of it with my next destinations as my playground. What started as capturing breathtaking landscapes during my travels evolved into something far more meaningful – the art of revealing human personality through portraiture.

I followed light across the world and found it in people.
